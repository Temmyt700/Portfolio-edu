Key Activities

African Border Day Celebration 2025

In June this year, Dr Olusola Akinbode participated at the 2025 African Border Day Celebration and also had the privilege of speaking on behalf of all Chairmen whose local government shares border with international countries in Nigeria.

International MOU Signing

On August 20th 2025, Dr Olusola Akinbode represented Ogun state and Nigeria to sign an MOU with Republic of Benin on boundary related matters.

Agricultural empowerment program

On the 2nd of June this year, the administration of Dr Olusola Akinbode in Yewa North local government flagged off an Agricultural empowerment program for small-scale farmers which distributed 11,000 cash crop seedlings to farmers in the council comprising of over 6,000 Cashew, 3,000 Cocoa and 2,000 Palm Seedlings.

Ministry Of Budget & Planning Town Hall Meeting

On Tuesday 21st of October, Dr Olusola Akinbode participated at the Ogun State Ministry of Budget and Planning Town Hall Meeting on the 2026-2028 Medium Term Expenditure Framework (MTEF) budget 2026. Dr. Olusola Akinbode spoke on behalf of all Chairmen from Yewa division.
